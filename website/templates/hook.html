The Lead Hook - 3

The lead hook is an excellent weapon, thrown with the lead hand around the guard in a tight arc or a wide arc depending on your position.
Notable Users: Julio Cesar Chavez, Oscar De La Hoya, Joe Frazier

The Rear Hook - 4

A powerful punch generally thrown in close range from the rear hand.
Notable Users: Mike Tyson, Saul Canelo Alvarez, Riddick Bowe

American Hook

A hook where the fist is oriented such that the thumb faces up and the palm faces the user.
Notable Users: Mike Tyson, Floyd Mayweather, Roy Jones Jr.

Casting Punch or Thai/Russian Hook

A hook where the thumb is angled downward and the palm faces slightly outward or away from the boxer. Distinct from Corkscrew sometimes looks more like a looping punch, torque doesnt drive this punch it comes mostly from distance travelled as well as a drop step or crouch.
Notable Users: Gennady Golovkin, Jack Dempsey

Check/Pivot Hook

The check hook is an important weapon for pure boxers; it involves pivoting on the lead leg to the outside and throwing a lead hook , forcing your opponent to careen forward toward your previous position.
Notable Users: Floyd Mayweather, Terrence Crawford, Erislandy Lara

Corkscrew Hook

a hook thrown where more torque is emphasized where the thumb faced downward and the palm faces outward or away from the boxer.
Notable Users: Gennady Golovkin, Jack Dempsey, Gabriel Vargas (Kickboxer)

Double Hook

A series of hooks where you vary the power of the first punch to the head or body and follow up with another hook of equal or more power with the same hand to the head or body.
Notable Users: Roy Jones Jr., Saul Canelo Alvarez

European Hook

A hook where the thumb faces the user and the palm is downwards.
Notable Users: 'Smokin' Joe Frazier, Gennady Golovkin, Naoya Inoue

Leaping Hook/Gazelle Punch

A lead hook thrown while leaping at your opponent, while this hook is very powerful, it’s also risky if countered.
Notable Users: Mike Tyson, Floyd Mayweather, Floyd Patterson, Muhammad Ali

Liver Blow

A hook thrown from the left hand to the lower right side of the abdomen where the liver lies, a bundle of nerves that surround the liver make the area an important point to defend, a good liver blow will force even the toughest opponent to the canvas.
Notable Users: Julio Cesar Chavez, Tommy Morrison, Henry Armstrong

Shovel Hook or Mexican Hook

The shovel hook is a hybrid between a hook and an uppercut, the hook comes at an upward angle, generally thrown from the lead hand toward the liver or pit of the stomach.
Notable Users: Saul Canelo Alvarez, Julio Cesar Chavez

Throw-Away Hook

More of a touch or a slap than a punch, it’s used to disorient or occupy the opponents guard which you can then follow up with another hook with either hand or even an uppercut.
Notable Users: Saul Canelo Alvarez, Julio Cesar Chavez
